Computers!

I spend a significant portion of my time writing code and doing things with technology. I run a community of really awesome programmers which you should totally join. I like building things for the fun of it, and as a result have a huge amount of side projects that I don’t really know what to do with. I churn them out at such a crazy rate that there isn’t really enough space for them here, but you can click here to see an abridged list.

The languages/technologies I use on a daily basis and love are TypeScript, Rust, and Next.js. I daily drive NixOS with KDE Plasma, you can check out my dotfiles. I design everything I make, and some things that I don’t make, using Figma exclusively.

Last summer I rode across the country with a bunch of other cool programmers on a literal freaking train with a full-on superdome, so that was pretty cool.

Music!

I’m a very musical person. I’ve played cello in classical, jazz, and new music styles for almost 11 years. For a couple years I took piano lessons but I got a bit too busy to continue. Sometimes I compose music for acoustic instruments, and sometimes I create electronic bleep bloop sounds in a variety of styles using Ableton, REAPER, Pd, and Max. Sometimes I even mix acoustic and electronic!

I also listen to music for a large portion of my time awake. In fact, I’m currently listening to song by artist— this is live updated! I enjoy a lot of genres and artists, but I like upbeat and catchy things. I listen to a lot of hyperpop, various types of electronic dance music, and some rock. I also play some Beat Saber, although I haven't had enough time recently.

Enjoy some words I wrote about electronic music: Familiarity in Electroacoustic Music: A Comparative Analysis of Two Approaches
